HISTORY

Our journey began in the quaint town of Milan, Italy, where the seeds of education were planted by the visionary educator, Professor John Potens. Established with a commitment to delivering quality education to the local community, Potens Academy has flourished over the decades, adapting to evolving educational trends and embracing technological advancements.

The school became a hub for arts and culture, fostering a creative environment that produced several renowned artists and musicians. In the 1980s, Potens Academy embraced diversity, becoming a pioneer in inclusive education. As the years passed, the school's alumni achieved success in various fields, contributing to the town's prosperity.
